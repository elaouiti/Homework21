Training Makes a Happy Dog

Training is an important part of having a dog and creating a happy healthy environment for your pup to live in. Training your dog keeps them mentally stimulated, and engaged with you because the training comes from you. You'll find that your dog looks at you more often for direction and guidance, which enriches the bond that you share. Physical exercise keeps your dog physical engaged without stress, meaning the your dog won't feel the need to chew or destory the things in your house. Lastly, all the treats your dog gets from training will keep them satisfied.
